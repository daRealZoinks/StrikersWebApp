How do I get started?

First, you need to download the game. Then, you can start playing.

How do I create a game?

Once you entered the game, you can create a game by clicking the "Create Game" button on the top left of the screen.

How do I join a game?

Once you entered the game, you can join a game by entering the game code in the "Join Game" field on the top left of the screen.
